About Janki Panchal

Ms. Janki Panchal is a Life coach since 2009. She is a Tarot Card expert reader and trainer, Vastu consultant, Astrologer, Pranic Healer - Pranic psychotherapist (World pranic healing foundation, Philippines), Crystal therapist, Neuro-linguistic programming practitioner, Angel therapist, Motivational speaker. Over the experience of 13 years, Janki Panchal got the chance to analyse many individuals as well groups of people and gather diverse knowledge in different areas which enabled her to unlock the inner potential to achieving success in all areas of life, including health, wealth, career, relationships, family, academics and finance. She has always a strong sense of purpose and direction which leads her to choose a profession that has the potential to expand consciousness and transform generation into their higher self. Janki has a unique and fresh perspective of seeing life which mesmerises people to lead a healthy and positive life. When it comes to the transformation of the client’s life she is always honest and clear about her thoughts plus she maintains privacy. In the past 13 years, She has done numerous events on all modalities in various cities where maximum people attended her workshops & events. As a Vastu consultant, she has visited numerous architectural structures such as buildings, houses, factories, orphanages, shops & offices for Vastu correction & its remedies.She reads the energy of infrastructure and its impacts on it and balances the right amount of energy using Crystals and Vastu objects. She counselled countless people in one on one sessions of all modalities. She has been invited by many schools and organisations to deliver sessions on health, academic topics, memory techniques, goal setting methods, motivation, relationship, meditation & spirituality. In 2015, she was honoured as Tarot Expert by Panchal Seva Samaj (Bilimora) for contributing her knowledge in community through service. In 2016, She was awarded as Tarot Expert par excellence by ISHMA world foundation, Mumbai. In April 2019, she was honoured by Mr. Nastur Bejan Daruwala (Celebrity astrologer, Ahmedabad) for being a part of spiritual event name of Spiritual Togetherness in Bhavnagar. In September 2019, she was honoured by Smt. Shalini Agarwal (IAS, Collector of Vadodara) for contributing her knowledge in spiritual event name of Spiritual Togetherness in Vadodara.
